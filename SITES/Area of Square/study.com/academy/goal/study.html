Study Courses

Find a study guide to help you improve your grades, do better in school, or learn a new subject. Our library of hundreds of study guides covers topics in math, English, history, science, and more. Learn on your own schedule with our engaging, self-paced video lessons. Choose a course below and get started!

How it Works

  • Choose a study guide: Browse our library of study guides to find the topic you want to learn or review.
  • Watch fun and engaging video lessons: Each study guide consists of bite-sized video lessons that bring concepts to life and make them easy to understand.
  • Test yourself with practice quizzes and exams: See how much you've learned with the practice quizzes that follow each lesson.
  • Get extra help from an expert instructor: Our team of expert instructors is here to answer your questions.

Who Our Courses Are For

  • Students preparing for an exam
  • Teachers looking for resources to teach a specific topic
  • Lifelong learners seeking to learn about a new topic

Why Our Courses Work

  • Access to all study guides: Our study guides cover all subjects, including math, English, science, history, and more. Access them all with just one subscription!
  • Progress tracking made easy: Your personal dashboard tracks your progress through the study guide so you can see what you've learned and what you still need to master.
  • Practice problems and quizzes: Each lesson comes with a practice quiz so you can make sure you've mastered the material.
  • Expert instructors: We've found the best instructors in the world to teach our study guide lessons. Our expert instructors make topics easy to understand and fun to learn.
  • Courses for any goal: Use our courses to review a topic, get homework help, replace a tutor, or earn college credit.
